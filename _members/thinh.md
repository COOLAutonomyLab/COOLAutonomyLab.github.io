Thinh is currently an Assistant Professor in the Aerospace Engineering and Engineering Mechanics Department at UT Austin. He was born in Vietnam, where he got his bachelor degree in ECE at Hanoi University of Science and Technology in 2008. He was very fortunate to study in the undergraduate talented program (majored in automatic control). He obtained my master's in ECE at the University of Oklahoma in 2013. In 20218, he finished my Ph.D. in ECE at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. At Illinois, he was the recipient of the Harriett and Robert Perry Fellowship Award in two years, 2016 and 2017. After his PhD, he joined the Georgia Institute of Technology as a TRIAD postdoctoral fellow. From 2020-2024, he was an assistant professor in the ECE department at Virginia Tech. He received the 2024 AFOSR YIP and NSF CAREER Awards.

Hobbies: A big fan of football (or soccer), he plays almost anytime he can. He also loves to travel to explore new food and culture.
