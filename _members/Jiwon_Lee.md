Jiwon Lee is a Ph.D. student in Aerospace Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin. She received her both M.S. (2025) and B.S. (2022) degrees in Aerospace Engineering from Seoul National University. She previously worked as a robotics engineer at Samsung Electronics, focusing on developing dual-arm manufacturing robots. Her research interests include safe autonomy and reinforcement learning for aerial and robotic systems.
