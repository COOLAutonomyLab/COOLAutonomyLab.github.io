# Real-Time Decision Making for Autonomy

The Control, Optimization, and Online Learning (COOL) for Autonomy lab at the [University of Texas, Austin](https://www.ae.utexas.edu) focuses on developing advanced real-time decision-making strategies for autonomy to complement humans in performing complex tasks. We are very interested in any opportunities to educate and train the next generation of scientists, researchers, and engineers in this rapidy advancing field. Together as a team we are definitely COOLer, have more fun, and can do much better. [Our group member](https://coolautonomylab.github.io/team/)
